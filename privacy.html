Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

BIN DAUD ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your personal information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, password
  • Order Information: Shipping address, billing address, payment details
  • Communication: Messages sent through contact forms, emails, or WhatsApp
  • Newsletter: Email address when subscribing to updates

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Browser Data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited
  • Cookies & Storage: Using localStorage to store cart, preferences, admin state
  • Usage Analytics: Product views, time spent on site, click patterns

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for:

  • Processing and fulfilling orders
  • Sending order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Responding to inquiries and customer support
  • Sending marketing emails and newsletters (with your consent)
  • Improving our website and user experience
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, secure servers, and secure payment processing. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Cookies & LocalStorage

Our website uses localStorage to store:

  • Cart Data: Your shopping cart items and quantities
  • Admin State: Admin dashboard data (admin only)
  • Preferences: Your browsing preferences and settings

These are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly share them.

5. Third-Party Services

We use WhatsApp integration for order confirmation and customer support. WhatsApp's privacy policy applies when you interact with us via WhatsApp. We do not share your data with third parties except as required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@bindaud.com

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete such information immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@bindaud.com
  • WhatsApp: +92 328 858 2902
  • Location: Islamabad, Pakistan

By using BIN DAUD, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
