Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BIN DAUD.

📦 Products

What sizes do you offer? ⬇️

We offer sizes XS, S, M, L, XL, and 2XL. Each product has a detailed size guide in the product description. Check our size chart before ordering to ensure perfect fit.

What materials do you use? ⬇️

All BIN DAUD pieces are crafted from premium materials including 100% cotton, high-quality blends, and sustainable fabrics. Each product page lists specific materials and care instructions.

Are your products sustainable? ⬇️

Yes! We're committed to sustainability. We use eco-friendly materials where possible, minimize waste in production, and use biodegradable packaging for all shipments.

Can I customize or personalize products? ⬇️

Customization options vary by product. Contact us at hello@bindaud.com to discuss personalization possibilities for your order.

🛒 Ordering

How do I place an order? ⬇️

Browse our shop, select your items, choose your size and color, and add to cart. Proceed to checkout, fill in your details, and submit. You'll receive an order confirmation on WhatsApp.

Do you offer discounts or coupons? ⬇️

Yes! We regularly run promotions and offer discount codes. Subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on the latest offers. You can apply coupon codes during checkout.

What payment methods do you accept? ⬇️

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), bank transfers, and mobile wallet payments (EasyPaisa/JazzCash). Choose your preferred method during checkout.

Is my order information secure? ⬇️

Yes! Your personal information is stored securely and encrypted. We never share your data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

📦 Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take? ⬇️

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days across Pakistan. Express delivery (1-2 days) is available for Islamabad metro areas. Check our Shipping Info page for full details.

How much is shipping? ⬇️

Shipping costs vary by location: Islamabad (PKR 250), Punjab (PKR 300), KPK (PKR 400), Sindh (PKR 500), Balochistan (PKR 700). Free shipping on orders over PKR 8,000!

Can I track my order? ⬇️

Yes! You'll receive tracking information via WhatsApp once your order ships. You can also contact us for real-time delivery updates.

🔄 Returns & Exchanges

What's your return policy? ⬇️

We offer a hassle-free 7-day return policy. Items must be unworn, unused, and in original packaging. Visit our Returns page for complete details.

How do I initiate a return? ⬇️

Contact us via WhatsApp or email within 7 days of delivery. We'll provide return shipping instructions and cover shipping costs for valid returns.

What if my item arrives damaged? ⬇️

If your item arrives damaged, contact us immediately with photos. We'll send a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.
