Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about you, such as your preferences and browsing history.

How BIN DAUD Uses Local Storage

BIN DAUD primarily uses browser localStorage (not traditional cookies) to enhance your experience. localStorage stores data locally on your device and is not automatically sent to our servers.

Types of Data We Store

1. Shopping Cart Data

  • What: Items you add to your cart, quantities, selected sizes, and colors
  • Storage Key: bindaud_cart
  • Purpose: To remember your items if you leave and return to our site
  • Duration: Until you clear your cart or browser storage
  • Data Shared: Only sent to our server when you proceed to checkout

2. Admin Dashboard Data

  • What: Admin login state, products, orders, and settings (admin users only)
  • Storage Key: bindaud_admin_state, bindaud_admin_session
  • Purpose: To maintain admin dashboard functionality
  • Duration: Until logout or browser storage cleared
  • Access: Admin only

3. User Preferences

  • What: Your browsing preferences and site settings
  • Purpose: To enhance your user experience
  • Duration: Persistent unless cleared

4. Newsletter Subscriptions

  • What: Email addresses of newsletter subscribers
  • Storage Key: bindaud_newsletter_emails
  • Purpose: To manage newsletter communications
  • Duration: Until unsubscribed

How to Control localStorage

You have full control over localStorage on your device:

  • Clear localStorage: Open Developer Tools (F12) → Application → localStorage → Clear
  • Disable localStorage: Adjust your browser settings to disable local storage
  • Private/Incognito mode: localStorage is cleared when you close private browsing sessions

Third-Party Services

BIN DAUD integrates with WhatsApp for order confirmation and customer support. WhatsApp may use their own cookies and tracking technologies. Please refer to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy for more information.

Your Privacy Rights

  • You can opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • You can request deletion of your stored data
  • You can export your data at any time
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of BIN DAUD indicates acceptance of any changes.

Important Note

⚠️ Disabling localStorage may affect functionality:

  • Your shopping cart will not be saved
  • Your browsing preferences will not be remembered
  • You may experience a reduced user experience
